The thesis addresses the following research questions:
\begin{description}
  \item[RQ1] Under practical sequence-length limits of a transformer encoder, can the model support effective deinterleaving and recover operating modes via clustering?
  \item[RQ2] Does a joint objective that couples deinterleaving with mode-aware clustering yield better clustering performance or representations than a comparable single-objective transformer baseline focused on deinterleaving alone?
\end{description}
